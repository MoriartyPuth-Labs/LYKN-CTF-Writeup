======================================================================
MISS MY SCHOOL
======================================================================

Category: OSINT
Status: SOLVED
Flag: LYKNCTF{long_bien_elementary}


CHALLENGE DESCRIPTION
---------------------
> "Talking about home makes me remember my old elementary school. Using either point B or point C
> from the previous challenge (miss my home) as the starting point, my elementary school is
> located within a 2 km radius of one of those two points. I am 18 years old now, and I started
> first grade in 2013. Find the elementary school I used to attend."

Flag format: LYKNCTF{school_name_elementary} — lowercase, Vietnamese accents removed, spaces →
underscores. Example: LYKNCTF{ngoc_lam_elementary}.

Provided: a photo of a school gate/courtyard with Vietnamese banners.


WALKTHROUGH / REASONING
-----------------------

The challenge nominally depends on points B/C from a prior "Miss My Home" challenge (a 2 km
radius), but we did not have those points. Instead we solved it directly from the photo:

1. Read the details in the image. A banner over the gate shows a landline phone number:
   ĐT: (04) 38 750 348. Other banners: election slogan "...NHIỆM KỲ 2021–2026", and an
   "80 NĂM NGÀY THÀNH LẬP ĐỘI TNTP HỒ CHÍ MINH (15/5/1941 – 15/5/2021)" board — consistent with a
   Hanoi primary school ((04) is the old Hanoi area code).

2. Pivot on the phone number (a unique, searchable identifier). Searching
   0438750348 / 02438750348 returns the official Long Biên ward page listing this number for
   Trường Tiểu học Long Biên (Long Biên Elementary School), Long Biên district, Hanoi.

3. This is consistent with the "Miss My Home" area (Long Biên), so it satisfies the 2 km radius
   condition without needing the exact B/C coordinates.


ANSWER CONSTRUCTION
-------------------
"Long Bien" + "elementary" → LYKNCTF{long_bien_elementary}


TOOLS USED
----------
- Visual inspection of the photo (OCR by eye) for the phone number and banners
- Web search on the landline number → official school listing


REASONING NOTES
---------------
- Key move: a phone number on signage is a strong OSINT pivot — far more unique than a
  building's appearance. It let us skip the geometric "2 km radius from B/C" step entirely.
- The 2021–2026 election banner and the Đội TNTP anniversary board corroborated a Hanoi location
  and the ~2021 photo date.
